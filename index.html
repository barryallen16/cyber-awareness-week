Cyber Awareness Week

Knowledge is Your Cyber Shield!

About

Cyber Awareness Week is a dedicated initiative aimed at educating students about the evolving landscape of cybersecurity, potential cyber threats, and best practices to stay safe in the digital world. This event will feature interactive sessions, hands-on workshops, ctf challenges, and engaging activities to enhance participants understanding of cyber hygiene, data privacy, and cybercrime prevention.
Our goal is to equip students, professionals, and cybersecurity enthusiasts with practical knowledge and real-world insights into the importance of cybersecurity. Through a series of interactive discussions, scenario-based challenges, and live demonstrations, participants will gain essential skills to identify, prevent, and respond to cyber threats effectively.
Beyond just learning, this event fosters a proactive cybersecurity culture, encouraging participants to implement safe digital practices in their daily lives. By raising awareness and building technical competence, Cyber Awareness Week empowers individuals to navigate the online world with confidence and resilience against cyber risks.

Day 1

INAUGURATION CEREMONY
The Cyber Awareness Week commences with a grand inauguration ceremony, graced by the presence of a honorable (name) as our chief guest. As cyber threats continue to evolve, law enforcement plays a crucial role in safeguarding individuals and organizations against cybercrime. The session will highlight the importance of staying safe online and help participants understand how to protect themselves from cyber threats. This week will provide useful knowledge and skills to stay secure in the digital world.
STAFF AWARENESS SESSION
This staff awareness session is designed to educate faculty and administrative staff on common cyber threats, workplace security best practices, and data protection strategies. By understanding these risks and adopting secure online habits, staff members can contribute to a safer digital environment, both professionally and personally.

Day 2

LECTURE ON FUNDAMENTALS OF CYBERSECURITY FOR STUDENTS
This session introduces students to the basics of cybersecurity, including common threats, best practices, and ways to stay safe online. It will provide a strong foundation to understand digital risks and protective measures.
HANDS-ON PRACTICE
A practical session where participants will engage in real-world cybersecurity exercises. This includes simulating cyber threats, analyzing security measures, and applying defensive techniques to enhance learning through experience.
QUIZ
An interactive quiz designed to test and reinforce cybersecurity knowledge. Participants will answer for the questions on cyber threats, online safety, and best practices from the topics covered during the session.
SCENARIO-BASED QUESTIONS
Participants will be given real-world cybersecurity situations to analyze and solve. This activity helps in developing problem-solving skills and understanding how to respond to cyber threats effectively.

Day 3

WORKSHOP
An industry expert will lead this workshop, providing in-depth insights and hands-on experience in cybersecurity. Participants will learn practical techniques, real-world case studies, and security best practices to enhance their knowledge and skills.
GTA(GUESS THE ATTACK)
Participants will watch movie clips featuring cyberattacks and analyze them to identify the type of attack being depicted. This activity aims to make learning engaging by connecting cyber threats with real-world scenarios.

Day 4

CTF(CAPTURE THE FLAG)
A simple engaging 8-hour Capture The Flag (CTF) competition, where participants will solve basic cybersecurity challenges. This event will help them apply fundamental security concepts, improve problem-solving skills, and gain hands-on experience in a fun and competitive environment.
PRICE DISTRIBUTION
Winners of the events will be recognized and awarded prizes for their outstanding performance. All participants will receive e-certificates as a token of appreciation for their active participation in Cyber Awareness Week.
